Frequently Asked Questions

Tactical Emblem Hub is your go-to platform for managing and organizing Fire Emblem teams and strategies. Build custom teams, create detailed strategies, and refine your tactics to dominate the battlefield. Whether you're a casual player or a competitive strategist, our tools help you plan, track, and perfect your gameplay.

Sign-up for Tactical Emblem Hub and then log into your account. Afterwards, press the "Create a Team" button in the right-hand corner of the header and follow the instructions on the screen. First, you need to pick a Fire Emblem game, then a team from that particular Fire Emblem game, then you can pick characters (with or without attributes) for your team, and finally, you can outline a strategy for this particular team.
